Vehicle Overview

The B8.5 S4 — Audi's Sport Sedan Pinnacle

The B8.5 facelift brought revised LED daytime running lights, a sharper front fascia, and S tronic DSG availability — making this the most complete version of the fourth-generation S4. It combines everyday usability with genuine sports-car performance.

  • B8.5 facelift with revised front and rear styling cues
  • Drive Select with Comfort, Auto, Dynamic & Individual modes
  • S sport suspension with optional adaptive dampers (DRC)
  • Bang & Olufsen 505W sound system — concert-hall acoustics
  • Full leather S sport seats with memory & lumbar adjustment
